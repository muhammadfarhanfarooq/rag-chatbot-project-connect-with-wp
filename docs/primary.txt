OVERVIEW
Queen’s Online School offers a full British Primary education programme for students aged 7–11 (Key Stage 2). The programme is designed to provide a rich, engaging, and interactive learning experience through live online teaching delivered by fully qualified UK-trained teachers.

With small class sizes (maximum of 16 students), the school focuses on building a strong academic foundation while nurturing a love of learning in a supportive and flexible online environment.

KEY FEATURES
- Live, interactive online lessons
- Small class sizes (maximum 16 students)
- Fully qualified UK-trained teachers
- Timetabled classes designed for online delivery
- Personalised learning experience
- Progress and goal tracking
- Access to learning materials and recordings
- Flexible schedule for family and social time

WHY CHOOSE QUEEN’S PRIMARY SCHOOL
Queen’s Online School is an independent British international school offering high-quality education tailored to each student’s lifestyle.

The school aims to:
- Inspire students to achieve their full potential
- Foster a love of lifelong learning
- Develop problem-solving skills for real-world challenges
- Create a strong sense of belonging and respect among students
