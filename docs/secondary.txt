OVERVIEW
Queen’s Online School offers a flexible and comprehensive British Secondary education for students aged 11–16. The programme covers both Lower Secondary (Key Stage 3) and Upper Secondary (Key Stage 4 / GCSE), providing a strong academic foundation and preparation for future studies.

Students benefit from a structured yet flexible online learning environment with live teaching, personalised support, and access to extensive digital resources.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

Lower Secondary (Key Stage 3: Ages 11–14)
