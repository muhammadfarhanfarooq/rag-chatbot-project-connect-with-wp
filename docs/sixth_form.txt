OVERVIEW
Queen’s Online School offers a flexible and academically rigorous Sixth Form (Key Stage 5) programme for students aged 16–18. Students study A-Level (or equivalent) qualifications through live online lessons, preparing them for university and future careers.

The programme combines academic excellence, flexibility, and personalised support, enabling students to study from anywhere in the world while balancing other commitments.

KEY FEATURES
- Live, interactive online A-Level classes
- Flexible study schedules
- 24/7 access to recorded lessons
- Small class sizes (maximum 20 students)
- Expert subject-specialist teachers
- Strong pastoral care and mentoring
- Digital learning tools and organisation support
- Global online student community

WHY STUDY ONLINE SIXTH FORM
